Assessing the Quality of Netflix's Catalog

I conducted a data analysis project on the quality of the Netflix catalog, utilizing SQL to explore the data and Power BI to build a dashboard visualizing the ratings of movies and TV shows on IMDB. By investigating the number of productions available, the breakdown between movies and TV shows, and the average score and distribution of ratings, I was able to identify interesting trends and patterns in the content offerings of one of the world's leading streaming platforms. This analysis provides valuable insights into the quality of the Netflix catalog and identifies areas of strength and weakness. Check out the final dashboard on the Power BI section of my portfolio!
